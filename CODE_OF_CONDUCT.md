# Code of Conduct

Sideplane aims to be a small, practical, and welcoming open-source project.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent, but accept corrections.
- Keep discussions focused on the project and its users.
- Disagree with ideas without attacking people.
- Be patient with newcomers and early contributors.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, or personal attacks
- Discriminatory language or behavior
- Public or private intimidation
- Publishing private information without permission
- Repeatedly derailing technical discussions
- Any behavior that makes participation unsafe or hostile

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues, block users, or take other reasonable action to protect the project community.

If you experience or observe unacceptable behavior, report it to the maintainers through GitHub.
